Homemade

Bake this soft and deilcious bread at home with just a few ingredients.perfect for sandwiches,toast.,or simply enjoying with butter

Perparation time

Total: Apprximately 3 hours

Perparation: 15 minutes

Rising: 2 hours

cooking:30-40 minutes

ingredients

  • 3 cup all-purpose
  • 1 packet(2 1/4 teaspoon)active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water(about 110°F/45°C)
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil or melted butter

introction

  1. Activate the yeast:In a large bowl,combine warm water,suger,and yeast.let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  2. make the dough:Add flour,salt,and olive oil or butter to the yeast mixture.stir until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough:Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead for about 8-10 minutes until smooth amd elastic.
  4. Let it rise:Place the dough in a ligthly oilde bowl,cover with a cloth,and let it rise in a warm pkace for about 1-2 hour,until doublel the size.
  5. Shape and rise aging:Punch down the dough,shape it to a loaf an place it in a greased pan,cover and let it rise for another 30 minutes.
  6. Bake the bread:Prehaet the oven to 373°F(190°C).bake the bread for 30-40 minutes,or ontil the top is golden brown and it sounds hollow when tapped.
